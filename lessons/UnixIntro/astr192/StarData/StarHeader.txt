
BrightStars.dat - Stars that are bright in the sky
NearStars.dat   - Stars that are close to the Sun


 Num       RA             DE          Vmag   B-V   Parallax    uRA     uDec

 ---    h min s    deg arcmin arcsec   mag   mag     MAS     MAS/yr   MAS/yr

Num : Catalog Number (B) = Bright star, (N) = Nearby star

RA : Right ascension (sky coordinate) in HMS

DE : Declination (sky coodrinate) in DMS

Vmag : Magnitude in the V band (Apparent magnitude)

B-V : B magnitude - V magnitude

Parallax : Trigonometric parallax in milli-arcseconds (MAS)

uRA : Proper motion in RA (uRA cos Dec) in MAS/yr

uDec : Proper motion in Dec in MAS/yr
